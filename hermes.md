* URL:        https://hermesengine.dev/

* LOC:        169845 (`cloc include lib tools/hermes`)

* License:    MIT
* Org:        Facebook
* Standard:   ES6 (partial)
* Years:      2019-
* Tech:       optimizing AOT compiler to bytecode, SSA IR
* Runtime:    register-based VM
* GC:         generational GC
